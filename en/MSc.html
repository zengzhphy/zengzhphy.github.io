Small Lab projects

  • MOT lab: Perform spectroscopy of Rb atoms, and use this setup to control the frequency of the laser. Then use this laser to make a magneto-optical trap containing ultracold Rb.
  • Simulation lab: Gain working knowledge of QuTiP and use it to simulate the dynamics of quantum oscillators and cavity-QED systems. (QuTiP is an open-source code library written in Python to simulate the dynamics of open quantum systems. Over the last few years, this has become a widely used tool for groups ranging from university researchers studying fundamental quantum systems to teams in industry developing quantum technologies.)
